About Me

Hi! I'm an aspiring full-stack JavaScript developer, ready to make a dynamic career transition from tech support to the world of coding. With a strong foundation in problem-solving, empathy, and adaptability gained from my tech support background, I'm now dedicated to bringing those skills to the tech industry.

My journey has been shaped by resilience, diversity, and a commitment to giving back. I've volunteered with organizations like Hadley School for the Blind and First Tee, where I learned the power of mentorship and the importance of diversity and inclusion. I believe technology should be accessible to all, and I'm excited to create solutions that are not only inclusive but also empowering.

Currently in the midst of attending the Rithm School bootcamp, where I'm eagerly gaining the technical skills to complement my strengths and contribute to a more diverse, inclusive, and innovative tech world. I'm ready to make a positive impact, not just for myself, but for the communities I'm part of and the industry as a whole. Let's embark on this journey together, shaping a better tech future for everyone!
